Studenthood is built with one simple belief: campus life should feel connected, convenient, and community-driven. Every student deserves an easy way to access resources, meet new people, share knowledge, and navigate everyday campus needs ‚Äî all in one place.

As students, we saw a common problem: everything on campus is scattered ‚Äî notes in different groups, items in random chats, cab-sharing in private circles, and lost belongings hidden in 100 WhatsApp messages. Nothing was unified. Nothing was organized. Nothing was easy.

So we created Studenthood, a platform built for students, by students, designed to bring the entire campus experience together.
